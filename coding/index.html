HackUC IV

Time Capsule

(December 2023)

  • Placed first in best project for the hackathon.
  • The theme of the hackathon was: "Time."
  • Working with a partner, our goal was to develop a website designed for users to save their cherished memories to look back on in the future.
  • This website shows a demo of how the time capsule feature works, while the last capsule is locked with a countdown feature.
  • This project was coded using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. We also used serverless functions with Cloudflare Workers to save and load the time capsules dynamically.
  • Check it out on Github and Devpost!

Hidden Gems

(July 2023)

  • Given the task of recognizing a problem in society and designing a website to help resolve it, we designed this website to contribute to cultural appreciation.
  • Our motto is "be a traveler, not a tourist", which means to gain meaningful experiences during one's travels, rather than simply observing, taking, and appropriating.
  • This project features three cities we recommend to users, with various locations to explore during their stay.
  • This was coded using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Beyond Binary Visions

(October 2023)

  • The theme of the hackathon was: "Create your reality."
  • This website can act as a vision board for people aspiring to get involved in the field of computer science.
  • We placed a special emphasis on diversity, as we recognize that the field of computer science is especially intimidating for minorities; we want our site to be welcoming to all who are passionate about computer science.
  • Our project aims to encourage people to take charge of their own future and create their own reality, regardless of the gender and diversity gaps found in the field.
  • This was coded using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
  • Check it out on Devpost!
